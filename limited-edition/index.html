--- layout: page title: Cosmic Dreamer Music Limited Edition ---

Cosmic Dreamer Music invites visual artists we respect to create limited edition prints that will be released alongside 7 inch records by musicians that inspire us as part of an ongoing series.

Each limited print and 7 inch will have a edition run of 200. Posters will be printed on archival paper and signed by the artist. The 7 inch cover art will also be designed by the artist. Each release comes with a digital download code.

More news including the artist roster coming soon.
